The Institute of Science and Technology (IoST) under Tribhuvan University (TU) focuses on science and technology education and research. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. programs in fields like physics, chemistry, biology, and environmental science. IoST emphasizes research and innovation, supporting faculty and student projects. It oversees numerous affiliated colleges across Nepal, ensuring standardized curricula. Modern laboratory facilities enable practical learning and advanced research. IoST also promotes collaborations with national and international institutions, fostering academic exchange and innovation. Its mission is to advance scientific knowledge and produce skilled professionals in science and technology. For more information please visit our website ::::
